It is our responsibility to protect the planet in which we all live. Being ECOWISE™ is the best way to be part of the solution – and that’s exactly what our initiative and ECOWISE™ branded products help everyone to do. They spring from our deep commitment to a more sustainable future. They are also living proof that, with our broad range of technologies and sustainable focus, we are the ideal partner for successfully transiting to sustainable solutions for the future needs.

We are committed to improving the sustainability proposition of our products by taking action along the five high priority areas.

Here are the five sustainability pillars of allnex. Hover over any section for more info

Circular economy

We continuously look for innovative ways to reduce resource consumption, maximize their lifespan, and then finally recover and recycle them at the end of service life

Renewable sourcing

We believe in using finite resources sparingly and aim to minimize climate impacts by finding renewable alternatives for energy and raw materials

Energy efficiency

Our product and manufacturing methods are designed to utilize energy efficiency across the full lifecycle of a product

Safer materials

We are dedicated to substituting potentially harmful chemicals by making safer options one of our fundamental operating guidelines

Emissions reduction

We strive to decrease the emissions of volatile organic solvents throughout the product lifecycle to protect people and our environment

Sustainable development goals

allnex has adopted environmental, social and governance (ESG) practices in close alignment with the UN's 17 sustainable development goals (SDGs).

Our strategy has been to focus on SDG #7, #12 and #13 for maximum impact. These 3 goals particularly lend themselves to allnex's contributions.
